The Reading Room

Built by one developer, shelved like a hundred years of one.

Unblocked Library started as a place to stop losing track of small game projects between repos. Every entry gets a call number, a shelf, and a card — the same care you'd give a real collection.

Everything here runs in the browser, hosted straight from GitHub Pages. No installs, no accounts to start — just open a drawer and play.
